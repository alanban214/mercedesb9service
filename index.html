Overview

The B9 service covers multiple systems on your Mercedes-Benz. This includes changing both its engine oil and filter, as well as installing a cabin air filter replacement to maintain engine efficiency while creating an ideal indoor environment.

Spark plugs must also be replaced, and the technician drains and refills brake fluid so as to ensure hydraulic pressure can reach each wheel cylinder properly.

Engine

Mercedes engines rely on spark plugs that ignite fuel in the combustion chamber. Over time, spark plugs can wear out. Replacing them as part of your B9 service keeps your engine running efficiently. This service also includes replacing the cabin filter, which improves interior comfort and air quality by blocking dust particles. Additionally, brake fluid is inspected and replaced to ensure safe driving by maintaining hydraulic pressure for optimal brake performance.

Mercedes-Benz technicians perform B9 services by checking and adjusting all fluid levels, including transmission, radiator, power steering, and brake fluids, to enhance performance and prolong the vehicle's lifespan.

Check and replace these critical fluids regularly while also looking out for leaks in areas like your engine, cooling system and brakes - early detection can prevent long-term damage and costly repairs down the line.
